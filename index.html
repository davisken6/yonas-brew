About Us

Founded in 2021, we started as a small batch brewery, brewing at home. Yona's Brew started with our love of dogs and beer. Yona is a 5 year old pembroke welsh corgi with a heart of gold. At our brewery, all dogs are always welcome. We even have a "beer" for your pup! For every beer we sell, a dollar is donated to the Guilford County Animal Shelter We are happy to announce our new location on Elm Street in downtown Greensboro is officially open!
